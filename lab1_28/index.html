Salt and Pepper Co began with three young entrepeneurs named Garrett, Spencer, and Sam. These three young individuals started their life with no salt and pepper, and decided to take things into their own hands and make their own life worth living. Through the power of love, these young boys took life by it's neck and strangled it until it gave them the power they needed to make salt and pepper a reality.
